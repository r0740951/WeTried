We're always stronger together. Donation store HGH is open for collaborations with other organisations.

 PimperNieuw:

Pimpernieuw The VZW 'PimperNieuw' originated from Het Gouden Hart. The idea was to reduce the waste mountain even more and also to reuse the broken items that we (unfortunately) get brought in in the give-away store and to make something new, fun, special with them. We also noticed that many creative people who were no longer suitable, or were welcome for a permanent job , came up with a lot of creative people in their Keuze project, but who can still do something for their talents. And why should we not offer such people free goods to bring their creativity to the forefront in a cozy group, at their own pace? Meanwhile, there are already a lot of people at work at PimperNieuw, there are already many nice products made. Concretely, the 'Weggeefwinkel' keeps old fabrics and materials separate that the workers at 'Pimpernieuw'can "pimp" or upcycle.

 PlantAardig:

The plant shelter collects unneeded and neglected plants, takes care of them if necessary and then delivers them to a new home. Adoption plants end up in the shelter for various reasons. In addition to the plants that we find ourselves on the street, people sometimes donate houseplants because they grow too large, something has changed in a living situation or care for a plant is becoming too much. Specifically: the 'Weggeefwinkel' keeps plant pots, coffee mugs etc. separate for the plant shelter.

We are open for cooperation with various partners: schools, governments, social and ecological organizations, associations, companies, private individuals, ...
