Activities

As an organisation, we plan lots of fun activities for everybody.

Link to the eventpage on Facebook
Donation workshops

HGH organises on regular basis free workshops where everybody is welcome. Everyone who has a special talent, knowledge, a skill that he wants to share with the others can give a workshop or a lecture. The intention is to bring people together and inspire them. The workshops take place in "Huis Van De Wereld", Stationstraat 21 in Lommel. Stay tuned on our Facebook event page.

Donation festival

Every 3th saturday of September we organise together with 'Weggeefwinkel Olmen' a free donation festival in Olmen-Balen where we invite donation stores from Flanders to donate goods on one big square. The visitors may take the offered goods for free and can enjoy free acts and animation.

Donation days

Each year, we choose a location next to a town or city center where we give free goods. Saturday 26 may 2018 we gave-away clothes for te second time nearby "het Huis van de Wereld" in Lommel.

Repairbars/markets

In the support of ecological, sustainable projects we stand regularly with give-away stands on different activities. On this manner, we can share our message and spread it outside of our shop. It offers us the opportunity to meat other people and organizations who are working on sustainable projects.
