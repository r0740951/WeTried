Welcome to HGH!

If its useless to you it might not be useless to someone else. We at HGH try to give these items a second chance for people who need it in our donation store. By doing this we help the envoirement and we help people out who might need it. one man's trash is another man's treasure.

But why do we do this?

The world can provide for everyone but not for everyones greed. We mostly do it to help the environment but also want to make people think about the material.


But how does it work?

At openings hours you can donate and take items for freefrom the store.


What am i allowed to donate?

We accept most items, exept for larger items because we cant store them due to the size of our store.
