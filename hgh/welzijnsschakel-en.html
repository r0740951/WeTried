Well-being

Het Gouden Hart is the first Well-being Link in Lommel. Every person deserves respect and has basic rights in our society. That is the vision of the 'Welzijnsschakels' volunteers. In fact, they offer opportunities to people who experience losses through poverty or ethnicity. This happens with the meeting of people with and without poverty experience in their own neighborhood. At Het Gouden Hart this meeting is included in our give-away shop, where the meeting is central. There is a task to show that poverty exists. A pair of switches acts as a voice for people in poverty and enters a dialogue with management.
